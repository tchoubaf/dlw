about us

WELCOME!

The DLW Scholarship & Charitable Foundation was created in July 2006, to provide scholarships to academically eligible students preparing to attend Grambling State University and students currently attending the university. Funding for this philanthropic venture is derived from corporate and individual contributors, and through the Annual Black and Gold Scholarship Gala. The Annual Black and Gold Gala is the signature fundraising for the Foundation.
