You must Register to Review Scholarship Application

PURPOSE: To financially assist Houston area upper class students attending Grambling State University graduate.The funds will be presented in the form of a scholarship for Houston area students distributed by the financial aid office and applied to the student account.


ELIGIBILITY: You must be:

  • ➢ A resident of Houston or surrounding area
  • ➢ G.P.A. of 3.0 or above, or can be based on need if less than a 3.0 G.P.A. but no lower than a 2.3 G.P.A.
  • ➢ Sophomore and above attending Grambling University

    • REQUIREMENTS: To financially assist Houston area upper class students attending Grambling State University graduate.The funds will be presented in the form of a scholarship for Houston area students distributed by the financial aid office and applied to the student account.

  • 1. Click on the button below and start an application.
  • 2. Include one letter of recommendation from your Dean or Professor.
  • 3. Submit most recent official transcript from Grambling. Must be mailed to foundation with the official school seal on it.
  • 4. Proof of need provide financial aid award letter.
  • 5. Include an essay no more than two pages.
  • 6. A wallet size photo attached to the application.
  • 7. Mail completed application to: DLW Scholarship & Charitable Foundation, Inc. Attn: Scholarship Committee 8325 Broadway Ste. 202 #119 Pearland, Texas 77581

    • No applications postmarked after that date will be accepted.Please use the contact us page for any questions regarding the application.
